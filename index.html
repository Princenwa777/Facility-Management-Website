overview

Nigeria Cleaning Service Ltd. continually focuses on creating the absolute best
place to work in the industry. Our culture is characterized by a workforce that
holds the highest standards of ethics, integrity, focused client care, and
commitment to our Core Values: Respect, Responsibility, Caring, Excellence,
Integrity, and Innovation.

In 2003, Cleaning Service is the foremost cleaning company in Nigeria,
providing cleaning services to local companies, blue chip companies and
providing Crude Oil Storage Tank Cleaning for the oil Companies in Nigeria and
West Africa in 2004. We believe cleaning is not just a practice it is our passion to
deliver excellent services to our clients.
